SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES
  • Empower women with limited resources to reach their full potential.
  • To become a large-scale sustainable platform that provides resources and relevant and transformative tools to the women of Latin America throughout their lives.
  • Create supportive spaces that women need to break the cycle of poverty and transform their life and that of their family.
  • Manage projects that contribute to the improvement of the quality of life of women, children, teenagers, youth, the family and therefore social and community development.
